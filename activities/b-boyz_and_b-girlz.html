Call it breakdancing, boogie, or urban acrobatics, The Spot is the place to "B" with one 8' x 12' and two 12' x 12' lineulum floors in our breakdance studio. We often host 30 to 40 b-boyz, b-girlz, and bystanders and have frequent dance contests for cash prizes. The Spot occasionally sponsors b-boyz and b-girlz to attend national and international hip-hop summits and breakdancing conventions.
