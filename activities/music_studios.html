The Spot's four pre-production studios contain Alesis 6.0/6.1 keyboards integrated with Micrologic sequencing software. The pre-production studios are where Spot participants make beats and rhythms. We train them how to operate the keyboards and sequencing software. Eventually, the songs are transferred to DAT (Digital Audio Tape), mastered on SoundEdit/SoundDesigner 2 recording/editing software, and finally burned to CD.

In our SOC multitrack studio Spot participants lay vocal and instrumental tracks. We have a DA-38 multitrack recorder and an ASR-10 sampling keyboard. The studio has voice processing capabilities that can manipulate and process the singer's voice.

Youth often create covers for their albums in our computer graphics lab.
