B-BUY is a collaborative effort between The Spot youth center and Microbusiness Development Corporation to strengthen, encourage, and promote the businesses created and operated by young entrepreneurs in Denver's urban settings.

The program includes community mentors, business plan assistance, professional networking, basic finances, and a small loan pool overseen by a committee of youth and community members.
