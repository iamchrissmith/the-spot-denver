Collaborating with the U.S. Department of Labor and the Mayor's Office of Workforce Development, The Spot youth center has two Youth Opportunity Coaches on staff. These YOCoaches provide Spot youth with career counseling, a formalized pre-employment training course developed by the Piton Foundation, as well as part-time and full-time job referrals.

Check out the Youth Opportunity Movement online.

Other Denver organizations also partnering with the YO Program are:

  • Beacon Project
  • Community College of Denver Tech East
  • Curtis Park Community Center
  • Eastside Clinic
  • Manual High School
  • YouthBiz
