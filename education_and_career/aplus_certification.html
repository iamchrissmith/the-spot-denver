The Spot youth center's A+ Computer Certification Class meets in four week intensive blocks. In the first four week course youth focus on the hardware core section of the test. During the second four week section youth focus on the operating system piece of the exam. The classes meet from 6 to 10 pm Sunday through Thursday.

A+ is a nationally recognized certification for computer and technology professionals. A+ certified individuals are able to take apart and reassemble computers and diagnose software and hardware problems.
