  • YouthBiz
    The mission of YouthBiz is to unleash the potential of Denver inner-city teens to invest in themselves and their community. Through hands-on experience in 1) leadership practice, 2) starting & managing youth-run enterprises, and 3) academic advancement, these young adults will develop long-term transferable job skills, contribute ethical leadership and initiate socially responsible small businesses in their community.
