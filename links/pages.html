The Spot helps youth publish their web sites by providing access to computers, web publishing software, coaching on that software, and space to host their sites under the Spot's domain name.

The words, images, and opinions expressed in participants' web sites are not necessarily espoused by The Spot, Inner Places, Inc., Spot staff, volunteers, members of the board of directors, or The Spot's financial and community supporters, but rather are the sole artistic responsibility and expressions of the site author(s).
