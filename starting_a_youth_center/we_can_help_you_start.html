Presentations/Workshops

Our presentations and workshops draw from our experiences with The Spot and working to assist other communities with their start-up efforts. Any presentation fees are paid to our non-profit corporation. Please email or call us with any questions or suggestions.

  • "Are Adolescents Welcome? Creating Your Place for Youth."
    A motivational keynote address that helps community leaders recognize the needs, debunk the myths, offer real solutions, and inspire action. 30-45 minutes.

  • "Creating Your Place for Youth: A Hands on Workshop"
    A practical workshop which will walk your group through the process of identifying your needs, overcoming the obstacles, and beginning or advancing your start-up process. Slide presentation, sample forms, worksheets, resource lists, etc. are included. 1 hour to a full day.
