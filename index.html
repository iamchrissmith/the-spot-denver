The Spot youth center is downtown Denver's award-winning, nonprofit, drop-in urban youth center. We are open and FREE of charge to all youth ages 14 to 24.

Our Youth Center's Mission: To help at-risk urban youth, teens, and adolescents have fun and mature into productive adults by providing a safe, supportive night time youth center that encourages respect, creativity, education, employment, and career and youth development.
